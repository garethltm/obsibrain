## Design
- Uses sense of touch
- Can be combined into [[Virtual Reality (VR)]]
- User receives [[feedback]] via tactile sensation
## Issues
- Sense of touch very complex
	- Different nerves have different functions
	- Pressure
	- Temperature
	- Muscle
	- Hair movement
- Texture of surface particularly difficult
